# Chapter 6: Hygiene and Grooming

## Learning Objectives
- Provide safe and respectful bathing assistance
- Maintain skin integrity and observe for changes
- Perform personal hygiene care with dignity
- Prevent pressure injuries
- Adapt hygiene care to resident needs
- Use proper infection prevention during care

---

## 6.1 Bathing and Skin Care
Hygiene keeps residents comfortable and lowers infection risk.

Bathing considerations:
- Provide privacy and warmth
- Cover the resident except for the area being washed
- Use gentle soap and avoid harsh scrubbing
- Wash from clean to dirty areas
- Inspect skin for redness, irritation, or breakdown

Proper drying helps protect the skin and prevent irritation.

---

## 6.2 Oral Hygiene
Oral care is essential for comfort and health.

Include:
- Brushing teeth or dentures
- Cleaning gums and tongue
- Rinsing the mouth
- Caring for dentures with proper storage
- Reporting bad breath, sores, or bleeding gums

Good oral care supports nutrition and overall wellness.

---

## 6.3 Nail and Foot Care
Nail care should be gentle and attentive.

Guidelines:
- Clean under nails carefully
- Keep nails trimmed to a safe length
- Avoid cutting cuticles unless trained
- Observe feet for redness, swelling, or blisters
- Report any problems to the nurse

Foot care is especially important for residents with diabetes.

---

## 6.4 Perineal Care
Perineal care preserves comfort and prevents infection.

Remember:
- Provide privacy and explain the procedure
- Use gloves and clean technique
- Clean front to back for female residents
- Clean around the penis and scrotum for male residents
- Change gloves and wash hands after care

Proper perineal care protects skin and reduces odor.

---

## 6.5 Grooming and Personal Appearance
Help residents maintain their normal routine.

Assist with:
- Hair care and combing
- Shaving or beard care (as directed)
- Dressing in clean clothing
- Applying lotion or lip balm as needed
- Respecting personal preferences and cultural grooming habits

Promoting personal grooming supports dignity and self-esteem.
